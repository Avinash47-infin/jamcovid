3/7 Health Status / Questionnaire

Public health and safety are Jamaica’s top priority. Help us protect visitors and the Jamaican public by providing truthful and complete answers to the following questions.

The health authorities require that you get tested for COVID-19 PCR or Antigen at a CLIA registered or ISO 15189 certified laboratory. If your travel date is up to March 10, 2021, your test must have been within 10 days of your date of travel. If your travel date is on or after March 11, 2021, your test must have been within 3 days of your date of travel. The test must be authorized by the United States Food and Drug Administration (FDA) under an Emergency Use Authorization (EUA) or meets the standards of the World Health Organisation (WHO). A negative test result must be presented to an airline representative in order to complete the check in for your flight.

The test result should show the following:

  • Name of individual tested
  • Date of birth
  • Lab identification and credentials
  • Date the test sample was collected
  • Type of test (PCR or Antigen)
  • Test result
